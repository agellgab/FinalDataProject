Salaries of the World

Have you ever wondered how much money a president or a prime minister makes?

Check it out now! You have four options to compare the world leaders' salary with other interesting data!

World Leader vs World Leader lets you compare the salaries of world leaders amongst them.

World Leader vs Average National lets you compare the salary of a world leader with the national average wage of his/her country.

World Leader vs Other nation lets you compare the salary of a world leader with the average national wage of another country.

World Leader vs You lets you stand against the world leaders' salaries. Will you beat them?

Once you're done, check out the general overview. Wanna find out who's the world leader that brings the biggest bacon home?

The econonmies with the highest world leaders' salaries over its compatriots

The chart below shows you who are the presidents who earn the most if compared to the average national wage of his/her own country in on axis. The president of Azerbaijan earns 31 times more than the national average wage of that country, for example.

That same chart shows you what is the Gini coefficient of the countries in the other axis. The Gini coefficient is an index that measures the distribution of wealth in a country. The higher it approaches to 1, the higher the inequality in that country. South Africa has one of the highest Gini ratios, for example.
